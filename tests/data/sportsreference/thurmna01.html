What position did Nate Thurmond play?

Center and Power Forward.

When did Nate Thurmond retire?

Nate Thurmond last played in 1977.

What did Nate Thurmond average?

Nate Thurmond averaged 15.0 points, 15.0 rebounds, and 2.7 assists per game.

How many rings does Nate Thurmond have?

Nate Thurmond won 0 championships.

What schools did Nate Thurmond attend?

Nate Thurmond attended Central in Akron, Ohio and Bowling Green.

What are Nate Thurmond's nicknames?

Nate the Great, Chairman of the Boards are nicknames for Nate Thurmond.
